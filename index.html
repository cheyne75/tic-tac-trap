How to Play

Tic Tac Trap is tic-tac-toe with a twist: pieces come in three sizes and can stack on top of each other.

Setup

Each player has 6 pieces: 2 small , 2 medium , and 2 large (red vs blue). The board is a 3×3 grid.

On Your Turn

Do one of the following:

  • Place a piece from your hand onto an empty space.
  • Stack a piece from your hand on top of any smaller piece already on the board — yours or your opponent's.
  • Move one of your pieces that's visible on the board to a different space (empty or containing a smaller piece).

Winning

Get three of your pieces in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Only the top piece in each space counts.

Watch Out!

Moving a piece off a space can reveal a hidden piece underneath. If that completes your opponent's row, they win — not you! Think carefully before moving.
